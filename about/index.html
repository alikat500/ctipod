About

Welcome to my Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) weblog.

This space was created to explore and share the evolving landscape of cyber threats through the lens of CTI. As a cybersecurity professional, I launched this blog to combine continuous learning with practical application—both for myself and for peers in the field.

The goal is simple: make threat intelligence more accessible, actionable, and relevant—whether you're a SOC analyst, threat hunter, business executive, or someone curious about digital risks.

Here, I share insights on threat actor tactics, campaign analysis, strategic implications, and practical techniques for applying CTI in real environments. Posts may range from breakdowns of recent incidents and APT activity to reflections on building a threat-informed defense.

CTI is no longer a niche function—it’s central to modern cybersecurity. By understanding adversary behavior and translating it into operational awareness, we empower defenders to stay ahead of emerging threats.

This weblog serves as both a personal learning journal and a resource for the broader community. It aims to bridge the gap between technical depth and real-world impact, offering content that informs, challenges, and supports thoughtful security practice.

Whether you're here to expand your threat knowledge, gain context behind the noise, or contribute to the conversation—welcome. Let’s learn, adapt, and stay resilient together.

Stay vigilant. Stay informed.
